About Me

Developer | Writer | Educator


Rudi (he/him) is a Front-End Web Developer that specializes in designing dynamic and unique websites for small businesses and arts organizations. Rudi's development philosophy balances your business or organization goals with a design that is elegant, unique, and effective.


Rudi is currently completing a PhD in English from the University of California, Riverside. His dissertation project, "Smoke and Mirrors: Queer of Color Aesthetics from the Harlem Renaissance to the Present" is an analysis of experimental American artists intervening in discourses of aesthetic form, social power, and embodied difference. His writing on contemporary art has been published on KCET's Artbound and in the catalogue for the landmark 2017 art exhibition, Mundos Alternos.


Rudi teaches university classes on film, art, and American literature. Rudi's pedagogy foregrounds the role of desire and pleasure in any analysis of creative cultural production. Rudi's training and experience in teaching, research, and arts writing gives him a unique position to offer criticality to the conceptualization and implementation of dynamic, modern web design.
